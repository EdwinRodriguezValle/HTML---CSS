Welcome to the Peruvian Restaurant with positive impact on food quality, taste and security!!!

We use ecological certificate products to prepare our food.

A part of your bill goes straight ahead to fund small farmers who are working with us looking for future generation food security.

WHO ARE WE?

Pachamama Peruvian restaurant, is a product of a collective action of a group of Peruvian migrants who are living in The Netherlands. We aim to build bridges between Dutch and Peruvian society by bringing our foods and concerns to the city center of Utrecht. We strongly believe that a huge part of our culture is expressed in the food, taste and the future vision of our food security. Therefore, we work together with farmers who protect agricultural biodiversity and promote sustainable farming technologies.

WHAT DO WE DO?

  • We prepare and serve our food with just 100% ecological ingredients.
  • We make our best effort to produce as close as possible the Peruvian taste.
  • A group of our personal work 10% of their time as a volunteers.
  • We provide support to Peruvian farmers who works with us.
  • We found agricultural projects that aim to preserve certains not commerical crops.
  • We create new recipiest with the news products produced by our partners.
